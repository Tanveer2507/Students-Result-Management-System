SRMS is a comprehensive web application built with Django for managing student results, attendance, assignments, and academic records. It includes separate portals for Admins, Teachers, and Students.

Admin Portal

Complete management of students, teachers, classes, and results

Teacher Portal

Attendance, assignments, marks entry, and student tracking

Student Portal

View results, attendance, submit assignments, download marksheets
